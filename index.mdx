## What is AutoQuill AI?

AutoQuill AI is a system-wide audio transcription application for macOS that converts your speech into text anywhere on your computer. Press a global hotkey in any app — your code editor, email client, document editor, or chat window — speak, and your words appear as text instantly.

It supports three distinct transcription modes for different workflows:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Standard Transcription" icon="toggle-on" href="/features/standard-transcription">
    Press once to start, press again to stop. Text is copied and ready to paste.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Push-to-Talk" icon="hand" href="/features/push-to-talk">
    Hold a key while speaking. Release to transcribe instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Assistant Mode" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/features/ai-assistant-mode">
    Select text, speak instructions, and AI refines or generates content for you.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## System requirements

| Requirement | Minimum |
|---|---|
| macOS | 10.14 (Mojave) or later |
| RAM | 4 GB (8 GB recommended) |
| Storage | 100 MB |
| Microphone | Required |
| Internet | Required for AI transcription |

<Note>
  AutoQuill AI requires an API key from [Groq](https://console.groq.com) for AI-powered transcription. The free tier is sufficient for most personal use.
</Note>
